I spent my 2023 Summer (May-August 2023) working at Lincoln Electric Additive Solutions. I also learned OpenGL and got incite into how it was used to render 3D and 2D graphics in the software. As a final project, I created a Windows application with C++ and OpenGL to allow my co-intern to view a 3D representation of his .ply files by being able to zoom in and out, and pan around the model.
